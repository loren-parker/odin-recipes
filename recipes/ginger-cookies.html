Ginger Cookies

An incredible treat for a cold fall day

Containing ginger, molasses, and plenty of tasty spices; these chewy cookies are my all-time favourite (don't be afraid to add extra spice)!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1 tbsp water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Set aside 2 tbsp sugar in a small bowl.
  2. In a bowl, sift together the flour, ginger, baking soda, cinnamon, cloves, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Beat in an egg, then stir in molasses and water. Slowly stir in the sifted ingredients until well combined.
  4. Using floured hands, shape the dough into 24 evenly sized balls. Roll each ball in the sugar you set aside, until coated. Place cookies two inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets, then flatten slightly.
  5. Bake in oven for 8-10 minutes.
  6. Allow cookies to cool for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
